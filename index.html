Philosophy on Technology and Innovation

My enthusiasm for technology and innovation is an expression of my belief in the transformative power of AI/ML and my gratitude that I get to be a part of this emerging world.

My philosophy focuses on three key principles that guide my work and interactions:

  • Clarity: How can we build and communicate in a clear manner?
  • Character: How can we conduct ourselves as we build in a way that reflects our values?
  • Change: How can we change people's lives for the better?
Answer these well, and everything else will fall into place.
